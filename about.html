About Us

At ElevateX, we bridge the gap between startups and researchers, fostering innovation through collaboration. Our platform allows startups to share their projects and connect with expert researchers, enhancing ideas with community-driven suggestions. We prioritize trust and security by requiring Aadhaar and PAN card verification for our users. Our mission is to empower startups by providing valuable insights and connections, ensuring that every venture has the opportunity to thrive in a supportive environment. Join us in shaping the future together!
